# Barrel Defense

A hyper-casual barrel shooter / horde defense game. Shoot barrels for upgrades. Shoot enemies to survive. The strategic tension: spend bullets on barrels (upgrades) or enemies (survival)?

Inspired by the "barrel shooter" genre from mobile game ads — except this one actually exists.

**[Play Now](https://human-pages-ai.github.io/number-runner/)**

## How to Play

- **Viking hordes** march toward your defense line from the far end
- **Barrels** roll in alongside enemies — shoot them open for rewards:
  - Red glow: **Damage up** (stronger bullets)
  - Blue glow: **Fire rate up** (faster shooting)
  - Green glow: **New ally** (extra shooter joins your squad)
  - Gold glow: **Coins** (spend in the upgrade shop)
- **Enemies** that reach your line kill one of your squad members
- **Squad wiped out** = game over
- Between waves, spend coins on permanent upgrades

## The Strategic Choice

Every bullet aimed at a barrel is a bullet NOT aimed at an approaching enemy. Do you gamble on the upgrade barrel while vikings close in? That's the core tension.

## Controls

| Input | Action |

## Difficulty

Choose Easy, Normal, or Hard on the start screen. Affects enemy speed, HP, spawn rate, and coin rewards.

Open `index.html` in any browser. No build step, no dependencies.
